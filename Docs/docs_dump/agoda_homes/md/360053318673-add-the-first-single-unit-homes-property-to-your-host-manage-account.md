# Add the first single-unit Homes property to your Host Manage account

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360053318673-Add-the-first-single-unit-Homes-property-to-your-Host-Manage-account

Updated: 2024-08-28T11:20:32Z

You will need to add the following details on the **Basics** and **Location** pages.

**Property type:** Choose one of the property types that best describe your accommodation. We support 4 property types: entire apartment, entire bungalow, entire house, villa.

**Property size: **Enter the property size in square meters, rounded to the nearest whole number.

If your property is 24.8 square meters, enter 25 square meters.

Conversion: 1 square foot = 0.093 square meters.

**Maximum occupancy:** Select the maximum number of guests that can sleep comfortably given the total bed space and sofas.

**Number of bathrooms and bedrooms:** Select the number of available bathrooms and bedrooms at your property. This is critical information for our travelers when choosing a property.

**Bedding configuration: **Once you have keyed in the number of bedrooms, you will see options to specify bed configuration in each of the bedrooms (including common space). Enter the count and type of beds for each bedroom (including common space). There are multiple bed types you can select: single, semi-double, double, queen, king, super king, bunk bed, sofa bed, and futon. You may also add multiple bed types for each bedroom by clicking on **Add another bed type**. Click **Next **and move on to the **Location** page.

**Location**: Type in your street address, building, floor and unit number, country, state/province, city, and zip code here. If we are unable to locate your property on the map, help up by dragging and placing the pin on the correct location on our map.

Note: The exact address of your property will not be displayed on our website. Exact location will be provided only after the guest confirms a booking.

Log on to www.agoda.com/homes. Go to the **Listings** page and click **ADD A NEW LISTING** to create a new property.

Once you have filled in the details on the **Basics** page, click **Next** and move on to the **Location** page.

To update these details (except property type and location) on the website after the property is live, go to the **Listings **page and click **Edit listing**. Then, go to the **Basics **page.

To create the first listing, log on to your Agoda app, go to the **More** tab to **Switch to host mode. **Click** List your place now. **If you already have a live property under your account, you may click on **+** button on the bottom right to add more properties.
