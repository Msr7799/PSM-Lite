# How to check your bookings

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360053665094-How-to-check-your-bookings

Updated: 2025-06-30T04:00:29Z

You can find the bookings your property receives on the **Reservations** page of the Agoda extranet.



**Desktop**

- Sign in to your account. If you manage multiple properties, select it from
**Listings**.
- Go to
**Bookings**.
- To view specific bookings, you can search by booking ID, guest name, stay dates, booking dates, or dates when bookings were last updated. You can also filter your reservations by room, rate plan, Targeted promotion, and payment model.
- Click on any booking to see more details.


**Agoda app**

- Open the Agoda app, switch to host mode, and tap
**Bookings**.
- If you have listed multiple properties on Agoda, tap
**All properties** and select the ones you want to view bookings for.
- To view specific bookings, you can search by booking ID, guest name, stay dates, booking dates, or dates when bookings were last updated.
