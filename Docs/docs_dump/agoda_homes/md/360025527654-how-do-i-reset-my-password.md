# How do I reset my password?

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360025527654-How-do-I-reset-my-password

Updated: 2024-08-28T13:59:48Z

If you forget your password or simply wish to change it, follow these steps:

2. Enter your email address

3. Click on the box provided next to **Verify that you are not a robot**

4. Click **submit **

5. Then you should receive an email from Agoda shortly. In the email, click the **reset password link **

6. Enter your new password and verify by typing it in again in the provided field

7. Click **change password **

8. Click **Back **to get back to the main login page. You can now sign in with your new password.
