# What are the different types of Agoda Homes listings?

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050964234-What-are-the-different-types-of-Agoda-Homes-listings

Updated: 2020-09-30T04:08:23Z

An Agoda Home is the entire accommodation listed by the property owner or the property manager who has the right to manage the ownerâ€™s property. Hosts do not stay on the property with guests.

**Multi-unit** properties: These are properties that have multiple allotments or multiple room choices available for guests to book. A property owner or manager needs to register and manage these properties through our YCS system.

**Single-unit** properties: These are properties with only one allotment. Only one booking may be facilitated in a single-unit property at any given point in time. Hosts need to register and manage these properties through our Host Manage system.
