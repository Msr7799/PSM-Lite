# Can I add a co-host to help manage my listing?

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360023675214-Can-I-add-a-co-host-to-help-manage-my-listing

Updated: 2022-12-15T10:12:25Z

Yes. For a variety of reasons, you may wish to have a co-host who can help manage your property or interact with guests. To add a co-host, just send a request through the contact form.
