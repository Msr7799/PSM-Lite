# Upload and caption your images

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360053496294-Upload-and-caption-your-images

Updated: 2024-08-28T14:14:02Z

When uploading your property images to Agoda, it is important to caption these images. We have hand-picked some captions that can be added according to the content of the images.

**Bathroom related**: bathroom, bathtub, shower, whirlpool bathtub. **Bedroom related**: interior, view, floor plans, room plan, family room, bedroom.**Essential amenities**: balcony/terrace, entrance, exterior, lobby, kitchen, facilities, outdoor pool, private pool, swimming pool, pub/lounge. **Others**: beach, recreational facilities, garden, surroundings, nearby attraction, map, fireplace, private beach, sauna, steam room, clothes dryer, public areas, gym/fitness.
