# If I have a booking request, does this block my availability for those dates until I accept or reject?

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360023682874-If-I-have-a-booking-request-does-this-block-my-availability-for-those-dates-until-I-accept-or-reject

Updated: 2022-12-15T10:16:52Z

Yes. You will have 24 hours to choose whether to accept or reject a booking. During that time, the availability of your property during those dates will be blocked. The block will expire after 24 hours, and the dates will become open again.
