**Property name: **Your property’s name/title is the most important. It should create the impression of a highly sought-after accommodation that meets the expectations of your potential guests.

Tips: Know your audience. Is your accommodation for business travelers, families, or romantic couples?

Examples: Spacious 4 BR Near Eiffel Tower – 7 min walk, Luxury Beach House Perfect for Romantic Weekend Getaway, Enjoy Sunsets at Fully Equipped 2BR Condo at the Beach, Rustic Private Cottage w/ King Bed + Oceanside View

**Property description: **Your property description is more than just writing about your bedrooms and bathrooms. It is about creating an exciting pitch to travelers to sell a once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Today, most travelers prefer to skim through online descriptions rather than reading a lengthy block of text. To make your description easy for guests to digest, break it up into chunks with clear topic sentences or sub-headers so customers can easily follow. Overall, the description of your home should be at least 120 words.

Only {distance} from the city center and {distance} to the {transportation options}, this (accommodation type) provides guests easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location and simple transport choices, you can easily access the city's must-see destinations such as {things nearby} and see more during your trip.

Guests will enjoy (x) private bedrooms and (x) bathrooms as well as a common living area with a view of {view}. All bathrooms are stocked with high-end shampoos, thick towels, and all necessary bath products so you don’t have to waste vacation time running to the store.

To update your property description on the website after the property is live, go to the **Listings **page and click **Edit Listing**. Then go to the **Description** page.

If you are creating a new property, move on to the **Description** page once you have finished adding your property’s **Location**.
