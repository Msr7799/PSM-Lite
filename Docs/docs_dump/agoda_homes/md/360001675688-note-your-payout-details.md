# Note your payout details

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001675688-Note-your-payout-details

Updated: 2024-08-28T12:45:03Z

If your payout method is set up as either Direct deposit to bank or PayPal, you can check your payout and earnings on Host Manage.

To see all payout transaction details, go to the **Listings** page, **Bookings & Earnings**, **Bookings**** **tab, and click **View t****ransactions**. This page displays all your bookings. The search tool can be used to filter payment transaction status and details.


August 7, 2020

Once a transaction has been completed, you will receive an email notification with the transaction details. You may also go to the **Listings **page, **Bookings & Earnings**, then **Bookings**** **tab and click on **View earnings. **

August 7, 2020

Note: You may check your payout details and earnings only on the web. This functionality is not available on the app.
