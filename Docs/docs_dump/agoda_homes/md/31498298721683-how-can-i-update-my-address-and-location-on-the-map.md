# How can I update my address and location on the map?

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/31498298721683-How-can-I-update-my-address-and-location-on-the-map

Updated: 2024-07-19T07:42:41Z

You can update your address via Host Manage. Once logged in, follow the steps in this article.

Update your location on the map by typing in the Search property’s location box:

Use keywords (e.g., area, state, or latitude & longitude) and select the most relevant results from the list that appears.

You can also move the pin on the map to your exact location

Scroll down to enter your property’s exact address.
