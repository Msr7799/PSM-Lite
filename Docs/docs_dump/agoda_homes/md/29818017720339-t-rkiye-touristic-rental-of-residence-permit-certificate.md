# Türkiye Touristic Rental of Residence Permit Certificate

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/29818017720339-T%C3%BCrkiye-Touristic-Rental-of-Residence-Permit-Certificate

Updated: 2024-06-04T07:37:43Z

In this article, you will find information on how to update your Touristic Rental of Residence permit number in YCS.

On 1 January 2024, Law no. 7464 on short term rentals took effect in the Republic of Türkiye.

This law requires partners renting entire residential properties for less than 100 nights to obtain a Permit Certificate for Touristic Rental of Residence from the Ministry of Culture and Tourism (“Konutun Turizm Amaçlı Kiralanmasına Ilişkin İzin Belgesi”).

We highly recommend you refer to the full text of Law n. 7464 here: https://www.resmigazete.gov.tr/eskiler/2023/11/20231102-16.htm

To comply with Law no. 7464, you are required to provide Agoda with your permit number or alternatively confirm that you are exempted from obtaining a permit in order to continue using our services.

**Could you please share more details about this law, and where I can find the official document?**

On 1 January 2024, Law no. 7464 on short term rentals took effect in the Republic of Türkiye.

Partners renting entire residential properties for less than 100 nights must obtain a Permit Certificate for Touristic Rental of Residence from the Ministry of Culture and Tourism (“Konutun Turizm Amaçlı Kiralanmasına Ilişkin İzin Belgesi”).

We encourage you to refer to the full text of Law n. 7464 here: https://www.resmigazete.gov.tr/eskiler/2023/11/20231102-16.htm

If you are unsure whether Law n. 7464 applies to your property, please seek independent legal advice from local counsel.


**What happens if I don’t provide my permit number? **

Listing your property without permit number may lead to fines to you and/or Agoda from the Turkish Ministry of Culture and Tourism. Law no. 7464 requires online travel platforms to display hosts’ permit numbers on their platform after 2 June 2024. If you do not provide your permit number to Agoda or alternatively confirm that you are exempted from obtaining a permit, we will remove your listing from our platform until we receive your permit number or confirmation of exemption.


**What should I do if my property if exempt from Law no. 7464? **

If your property is exempted, please confirm your exemption on Host Manage.

**What happens if I share my information after the deadline? **

After the deadline, your listing will be removed from our platform. We will display your listing again after we receive your permit number or confirmation of exemption.
