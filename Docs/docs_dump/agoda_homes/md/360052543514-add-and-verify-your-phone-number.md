# Add and verify your phone number

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360052543514-Add-and-verify-your-phone-number

Updated: 2025-05-14T07:53:00Z

Adding your phone number is an optional but recommended step to completing your host profile, ensuring we can get in touch with you quickly. You can add your number and verify it on the Agoda extranet using your desktop or mobile.


**Desktop**

- Sign in to your account.
- Open the account menu in the top right and select
**Profile **or click **Edit your profile**.
- Under
**Contact information**, select the country and enter your phone number.
- Click
**Save **to verify your phone number.
- Enter the one-time password sent to your phone and click
**Continue**.

**Agoda app**

- Open the Agoda app and switch to host mode.
- Navigate to
**More** >> **Profile** or tap **Edit your profile**.
- Under
**Contact information**, select the country and enter your phone number.
- Tap
**Save **to verify your phone number.
- Enter the one-time password sent to your phone and tap
**Continue**.


Once complete, your phone number will show as **Verified**. You can change your phone number at any time by following the steps in this guide.
