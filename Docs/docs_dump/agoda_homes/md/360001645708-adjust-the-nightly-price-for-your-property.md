# Adjust the nightly price for your property

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001645708-Adjust-the-nightly-price-for-your-property

Updated: 2025-05-14T08:03:12Z

You can adjust your property’s nightly price anytime after it’s listed, either through the Agoda extranet on your desktop or the Agoda app. You also have the flexibility to set extra guest fees to customize your pricing further.


**Desktop**

**Change the nightly price for all dates**

- Sign in to your account. If you manage multiple properties, select it from
**Listings**.
- Navigate to
**Rates and availability** >> **Pricing**.
- Under
**Nightly price**, enter your new rate.
- Click
**Save**.


**Adjust the nightly price for specific dates**

- Sign in to your account. If you manage multiple properties, select the property from
**Listings**.
- Go to
**Calendar**.
- Use the
**Selected dates** and **Specific days** to set the dates you want to edit.
- Set the
**Availability** to **Available** and enter the minimum price per night.
- Click
**Save**.


**Setting extra guest fees**

- Sign in to your account. If you manage multiple properties, select the property from
**Listings**.
- Click on
**Rates and availability** >> **Pricing**.
- Scroll to the bottom of the page and expand the
**Extra guest** section.
- Enter the rate per night for each extra guest.
- Under
**For each guest after**, enter the number of guests that can stay for the nightly price you set. The extra guest rate will apply to each guest beyond this number, up to the maximum number of people who can sleep in your place.
- Click
**Save**.


**Agoda app**

**Change the nightly price for all dates**

- Open the Agoda app, switch to host mode, and navigate to your listing.
- Tap
**More **>> **Rates and availability** >> **Pricing**.
- Under
**Nightly price**, enter your new rate.
- Tap
**Save**.

**Adjust the nightly price for specific dates**

- Open the Agoda app, switch to host mode, and navigate to your listing.
- Tap on
**Calendar**.
- Tap
**Edit** and use **Selected dates** and **Specific days** to set the dates you want to edit.
- Set the
**Availability** to **Available**.
- Enter the minimum price per night.
- Tap
**Save**.

**Setting extra guest fees**

- Open the Agoda app, switch to host mode, and navigate to your listing.
- Tap
**More **>> **Rates and availability** >> **Pricing**.
- Scroll to the bottom of the page and expand the
**Extra guest** section.
- Enter the rate per night for each extra guest.
- Under
**For each guest after**, enter the number of guests that can stay for the nightly price you set. The extra guest rate will apply to each guest beyond this number, up to the maximum number of people who can sleep in your place.
- Tap
**Save**.
