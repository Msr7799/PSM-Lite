# Other cleanliness resources for reference

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360052808093-Other-cleanliness-resources-for-reference

Updated: 2020-08-20T04:32:32Z

We've put together this article to help hosts on Agoda become familiar with cleanliness and hygienic practices recommended by your local authority. Hosts are encouraged to use resources from local authority as a reference in conjunction with Agoda's Cleanliness Guidelines when preparing your home for guests. It should be noted that this article is not updated in real time nor is it comprehensive, so it is important to follow local announcements and regulations to stay up to date.

Agoda's Cleanliness Guidelines can be found here.
