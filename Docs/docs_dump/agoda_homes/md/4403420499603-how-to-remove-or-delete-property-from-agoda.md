# How to remove or delete property from Agoda

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/4403420499603-How-to-remove-or-delete-property-from-Agoda

Updated: 2024-08-28T13:55:16Z

In the event you need to terminate your contract with Agoda and deactivate your properties, you can go to extranet directly.

Go to **Listings** tab and select **Edit listing,** then click **Deactivate.**


*Jan 14, 2022*


In cases when you are not able to honor any future bookings, you will be** required to find alternative accommodation for the affected guest**. If relocation is still not an option, you must compensate Agoda to cover the costs of relocation for those guests.

Shall you change your mind and want to reactivate your listing, go to** Listings** tab and reactivate them.

Note: You can deactivate your listing only on the web. This functionality is not available on the app.
