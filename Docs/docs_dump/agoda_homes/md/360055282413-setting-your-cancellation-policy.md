# Setting your cancellation policy

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360055282413-Setting-your-cancellation-policy

Updated: 2025-07-22T11:02:48Z

Flexible cancellation policies are a top priority for travelers when choosing where to stay. You can choose the cancellation policy that best suits your property, balancing guest expectations with your business needs. You can view or change your cancellation policy anytime on **Booking settings**.
