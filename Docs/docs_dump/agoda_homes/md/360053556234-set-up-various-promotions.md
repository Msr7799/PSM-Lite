Promotions are important to attract demand, increase bookings, and enhance your property’s visibility. With the Agoda extranet, you can easily set up or customize your own promotions to suit your business needs. Whether it’s offering special deals, discounts, or leveraging strategic placement and advertising, Agoda promotions help you capture the attention or bookings you’re looking for.

Agoda offers single-unit properties a variety of promotions to help you attract more travelers and maximize bookings. You can activate as many as you’d like from the Essential and Targeted promotion types.

Promotions can be viewed and managed on the main Promotions page. Currently running promotions will be listed under the **Active** tab while those that are paused, deactivated, or expired can be found under **Inactive**. Here you can see an overview of your promotion performance and settings as well as take actions like editing or creating a duplicate. Please note these actions may vary depending on the promotion type or settings.
