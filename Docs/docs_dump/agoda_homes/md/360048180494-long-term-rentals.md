# Long-Term Rentals

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360048180494-Long-Term-Rentals

Updated: 2022-12-15T09:59:59Z

Did you know that travelers can now book stays of up to 90 nights through Agoda? Set a monthly discount to attract these bookings and guarantee longer occupancy!

If you want to get the maximum advantage over your competition, we recommend setting your discount at 30% for a minimum stay of 28 nights. You can set your own Monthly Promotion by applying the Booking Booster from Host Manage **Overview** page

You can also create a Promotion in YCS (Go to **Promotions** page > **+Create a New Promotion** > choose **Long Stays** template > **Confirm promotion type** > on the Select date range section click **Show Advanced Features** > enter **Minimum Length of Stay**), and apply a custom cancellation policy and custom dates to any promotion.
