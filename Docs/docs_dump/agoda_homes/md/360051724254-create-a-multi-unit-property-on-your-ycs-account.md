# Create a multi-unit property on your YCS account

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051724254-Create-a-multi-unit-property-on-your-YCS-account

Updated: 2024-08-28T13:39:53Z

Create your YCS account by clicking on **Sign up**. If you already have an account, log in to add a new property.


September 4, 2020

From the drop-down list under your property name, select **Create new property**. Complete the registration form and click **Submit**. Upon completion, you will receive a confirmation email to your registered email address.

Sign in again and go to the **EC360 Dashboard**. Complete these 6 steps as outlined in this article. This must be done before your property can be activated and displayed on the Agoda website and app.

If you are creating a multi-unit property for the first time through your mobile, go to www.ycs.agoda.com and scroll down to **Sign Up for FREE Now**.


September 4, 2020

You need to have the **YCS companion app **installed on your phone in order to manage your multi-unit listings. Download the YCS app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
