# What is Child policy for Agoda Homes?

Source: https://agodahomeshelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/4416062968211-What-is-Child-policy-for-Agoda-Homes

Updated: 2023-01-10T08:47:24Z

Current trends show an increase in family bookings, we also discovered that booking with children allowed have a 48% higher ADR (Average Daily Rate) than a 12% lower cancelation rate compared to non-family bookings.

Now introducing Agoda homes child policy

Max occupancy now includes children as well. (max occupancy = Adult + Children)

To manage the max occupancy on the web after the property is live, go to the **Basic **page and select number of max occupancy on accommodates that you would like to adjust, and click save.




To update your max occupancy setting after the property has gone live, tap **Calendar**, tap setting, select pricing, update max occupancy number that you would like to adjust, then click **Save**.
