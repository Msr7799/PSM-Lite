If you want to review your earnings, Airbnb automatically generates your monthly and annual statements. You can access reports as far back as the year you started hosting, break down by listing and payout method, and download or email each report as a PDF.

For easy access, you have the option to receive reports via email or download reports in PDF format.

**Note**: When creating a custom report, you can also include optional details to review, like performance stats and payout method breakdowns.

The CSV file can be opened by any standard spreadsheet program (like Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or Apple Numbers). This spreadsheet includes additional information, like your earnings, Host service fees or cleaning fee (if you charge one), as well as gross earnings and any taxes withheld for each earning.

These reports can be automatically generated monthly and yearly (when available).
