**What is a Proactive Date Amendment Fee Waiver?**

- A simple tool that you can use to preselect your property’s policy on allowing date amendment. You choose the circumstance under which you will allow date amendment (if any).
- Any qualified situation that falls within the circumstance selected under the Proactive Date Amendment Fee Waiver will automatically be allowed through our system.

**Benefits to Property**

- Save time and effort while improving customer satisfaction.
- Substantially decrease the number of calls and emails between properties and guests, making the interaction seamless for you and guests.

**How to Set Up?**

- From the “Settings” screen, click on “Property Settings”

- Select or scroll down to “Optional Settings” until you see “Accept amendment fee waiver requests from our agents”

- If the box is checked for “Accept amendment fee waiver requests from our agents,” you will see a drop-down menu under “Auto date amendment fee waiver setting” where you can preselect your property’s policy on allowing date amendment
- The circumstances you can choose from include:
- Waive fee up to 24 hours before check-in date
- Waive fee up to 48 hours before check-in date
- Waive fee up to 72 hours before check-in date
- Waive fee up to 120 hours before check-in date

- By default, your property will opt into the following option:
- Waive fee up to 48 hours before check-in date

- You can opt-out from the program by unchecking the box for “Accept amendment fee waiver requests from our agents”


**Frequently Asked Questions**

**How do I opt-out, or make changes, to the circumstance that I am opted in to?**

You can opt-out or make any changes directly on YCS by following the instructions on this page.


**Can I quit the program?**

Yes, you can opt-out of the program on YCS by following the instructions on this page.


**Will I receive an additional amount if the new dates are more expensive?**

Yes, you will receive an additional amount equal to the rate difference between the original and new dates.


**How will I know when a booking is amended automatically by this tool?**

You will receive the date amendment details by email each time there is a date amendment fee waiver. The email will resemble a standard booking amendment confirmation email and will include the Booking ID, new check-in and check-out dates, and booking value.
