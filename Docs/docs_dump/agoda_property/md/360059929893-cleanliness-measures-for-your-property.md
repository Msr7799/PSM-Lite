**Importance of cleanliness measures to guests **

During the spread of the COVID-19 pandemic, we’ve seen a shift in bookings to move towards properties with higher cleanliness scores. The research revealed that cleanliness information will carry on being an important aspect travelers look for in the new normal style of living.

As a property owner, it’s crucial to showcase the cleanliness related measures you take at your property to build guests’ confidence and enhance their decision when booking. Your goal is to make guests feel confident that their stay will be as safe, clean, and comfortable as in their own homes. Our goal is to make it easy for you to show guests all the measures you’re taking to respond to the pandemic.


**Cleanliness and hygiene facilities**

Based on our research, we’ve created an effective and simple checklist of recommended cleanliness facilities at your property you can easily update on YCS as follow:


**Safety features**

-
**Staff trained in safety protocol**
- Arranging training to ensure property staff is aligned on the measures, safety, and health protocols e.g. wearing masks, understanding how to handle guests with risks.

-
**Contactless check-in/check-out**
- Online, auto, or self-service check-in to reduce human contact to avoid cross infection.

-
**Temperature check for guests and staff**
- Screening property staff and guests' temperatures when entering the building or facility.

-
**Daily disinfection in common areas **
- Disinfection in common area at least once a day.

-
**Daily disinfection in all rooms **
- Disinfection in all rooms at least once a day.

-
**Protective screens in common areas **
- Installation of a transparent protective shields in public areas between persons during face-to-face transactions.



**Preventive equipment**

-
**Face coverings on staff**
- All staffs are required to wear face shield and/or masks at your property.

-
**Sterilizing equipment **
- Equipment to remove, kill, or deactivate all bacteria, viruses, etc.

-
**Hand sanitizer **
- Providing hand sanitizers in property's public area e.g. at the entrance, lobby, restaurant etc.



**Physical distancing**

-
**Safe dining setup**
- Rearrangement of tables and seating in dining areas to ensure social distancing.

-
**Physical distancing of at least 1 meter **
- Social distancing measures enforced in common areas



**Health & medicine**

-
**First aid kit **
- Supplies and equipment which is used to provide medical treatment.

-
**Body thermometer**
- Equipment which is used to measure body temperature.

-
**Doctor/nurse on call**
- Property can contact doctors who can come to the property if needed.



**More tips on cleanliness measures you should take…**

- Add disinfectant when washing laundry and wash bedspreads more frequently.
- Sanitize electronics such as tablets, touch screens, keyboards, remote controls and in-property ATM machines.
- Ensure the sanitization and disinfection of publicly accessible toilets.
- Maintain and disinfect your swimming pools, as well as spas and other recreational facilities.
- Install air purifiers in common areas, and make sure to regularly change the filters.
- Offer cashless payment as an alternative option, if possible.
- Show guests what measures you’re taking on posters, TV, etc., throughout your property.
- Install hand sanitizing stations located in high-traffic areas throughout the hotel, such as the front desk, breakfast area, elevator lobbies, and fitness center.

Once you’ve provided us with this cleanliness information, you should be committed to:

- Keep records of all preventive measures and any changes that take place at the property.
- Update the facilities information on YCS. It’s important that your information remains accurate and up to date to ensure that guests are kept informed of your cleanliness and hygiene measures.
