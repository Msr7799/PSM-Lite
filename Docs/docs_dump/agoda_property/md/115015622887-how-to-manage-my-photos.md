**Overview**
**Having great photos is key to any property. Photos drive exposure, promote bookings and improve reputation. The Photo Page is an intuitive, user-friendly interface for you to manage and upload your photos. In this article, we will outline how to update your photos in YCS.**

Upload sections:

- Allows batch uploads
- Supports drag and drop function for uploading
- Allows maximum file sizes of 10 MB
- Allows max 100 MB per upload
- Minimum picture dimension is 1024x768 pixels
- Supported file formats are .jpg and .png
- Duplicated photo file names are not allowed


**How to update your photos?**

In Overview section you see all photos of the property and all photos for each room type. Missing photos and low-resolution photos will also be displayed here. You can easily assign photos to specific room types and tags/caption.

- The first image of the room photos will be the room cover photos on the front-end
- Photos will not reflect on the front end after uploading / or being deleted right away, due to the cache issues. It can take up to 6 hours before the changes are reflected on the website

**Frequently Asked Questions**

**Can I use the same photo for different room types?**

Yes! When you assign this photo, simply tick "Assign to Multiple" and choose multiple categories you would like to assign.

**Why my upload is failed?**

Please check the photos you are uploading meet the minimum requirements. Otherwise you can try to use different browser to upload photos. If it is still failed, you can contact our Accommodation
