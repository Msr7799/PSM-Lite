The safeguarding of customers and partners is of paramount importance to Agoda. We have been closely monitoring the Coronavirus updates and latest official travel advice to assess the impact on our customers and partners. At present, travels from, to and within affected regions has been severely impacted and prohibited in certain regions.

Following the latest official travel advice about the Coronavirus, it is unlikely that customers can travel to, from and stay at their accommodations in areas affected around the world. Aside from areas affected by the outbreak, travel bans announced by the government of specific countries or areas are also impacting guests traveling from all around the world.

The purpose of this article is to confirm which areas and period are within Agoda cancellation waivers amid COVID-19. This information is based on locally-sourced intelligence. For official travel guide, we recommend that you consult official government websites

**Other Areas Impacted by Coronavirus**

Customers impacted by the travel ban announced by the government (eg: bans on entry, mandatory quarantine on arrival, mandatory quarantine upon return home, or the suspension of on-arrival visas) or any other individual cases where customers are not able to travel (eg: due to quarantine notice/ area hotel shut down due to infection) are also entitled for the cancellation fee waiver.
