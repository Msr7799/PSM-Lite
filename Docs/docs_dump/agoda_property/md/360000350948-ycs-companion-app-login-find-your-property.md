# YCS Companion App -- Login & Find your property

Source: https://agodapropertypartnerhelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000350948-YCS-Companion-App-Login-Find-your-property

Updated: 2021-09-08T07:40:08Z

From Settings screen, you can view the current property you are working, change property, view YCS Companion App information, view terms and conditions, as well as Sign out from the application.

Settings page show you the current property you are working on.

To change property, tap on Change property menu and insert property's name or ID to search. Then tap to select the property from the list.

You can view information about YCS Companion App and view Terms and Conditions by tapping on About and Terms and Conditions button accordingly.

You can sign out from YCS Companion App by tapping on SIGN OUT button.
