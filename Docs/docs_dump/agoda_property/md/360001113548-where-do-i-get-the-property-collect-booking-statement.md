# Where do I get the Property Collect booking statement?

Source: https://agodapropertypartnerhelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001113548-Where-do-I-get-the-Property-Collect-booking-statement

Updated: 2024-08-28T12:43:42Z

##
|


To help your accounting department process the commission paid for Property Collect, Agoda is providing you with booking statements for completed payment batches in ePass. These statements will list all your Property Collect bookings in the respective payment batch and display both the amount the guest paid upon check-in and the commission we deducted for it.

To collect the booking statements:

No, the statement is only available for completed payments that included Property Collect commission.
