Agoda bookings are all instantly confirmed. Acknowledge Booking is a step Agoda takes to ensure that property partners are aware of the new booking that has been created. To acknowledge having received a booking, our partner should leave a remark for confirmation.


|
Not performing any actions on acknowledging the booking does NOT mean that the booking is not confirmed. The property is still expected to honor the booking as reserved by the guest. |
