# How do I contact Agoda?

Source: https://agodapropertypartnerhelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360055850733-How-do-I-contact-Agoda

Updated: 2021-10-04T04:03:52Z

|


The Help Center is built to serve as a library for Agoda partners to learn about Agoda systems and workflows which includes all the information needed to understand Agoda system settings, policies, and payment logic.


Should you need any assistance from the Support Team, you may reach out to the team via call or email stated on the booking voucher.


Our Customer Service Team is responsible to assist with booking related issues such as amendment, cancellation, guest contacts, and refund. This team is available 24/7 via call or email.

For email inquiry, you may send to biz@agoda.com


Our Accommodation Services team is responsible to assist Agoda partners with their inquiries on the YCS platform, payment, website content, and rate calculations. The team is available on Monday through Friday around the clock, except on public holidays.

For email inquiry, you may submit it along with your contact details through the Support widget located on the bottom right of the YCS (ycs.agoda.com) page once you successfully log in.

If you have any issue on login, please visit this article that can guide you on steps to be able to login successfully


Depending on the issue that you raise, you will be directed to the correct support team to assist you with the case.
