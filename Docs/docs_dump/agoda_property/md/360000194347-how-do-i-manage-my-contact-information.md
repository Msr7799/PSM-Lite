# How do I Manage my Contact Information?

Source: https://agodapropertypartnerhelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000194347-How-do-I-Manage-my-Contact-Information

Updated: 2021-09-22T06:12:53Z

|


You can manage your contact details on the Contacts page by going to the** Settings** Menu and clicking on **Contacts (NEW)** at the bottom of the menu.


This section explains how to create, edit and delete a contact. The description of how to set Responsibility and Notification and each field's details are provided later on in this article.

Note: The Contact details must be in English.


When you click on **Create New Contact** button, the form for input new contact will be displayed as in the screen below. If there are more than one email or phone number, please click **Add email address** or **Add phone number**. Click **Create** to save to information, or click **Cancel** to discard the change.

***It is recommended to put the phone or mobile number in international format without any spaces (e.g. Thailand (66), Bangkok (02), is input as 6621234567)***

If you want to update the Contact information, please click the **Edit** button. YCS will allow editing Contact details. Click **Save** to save the change, or click **Cancel** to discard the change.


You can delete a contact which is being displayed by clicking **Delete Contact** and confirm to delete the contact.


You can search a contact by name, e-mail, or job role using the search box on the top-left of the screen as in below picture.


You can sort the contact list by Name, Surname or Job Role using sort function under the search box as in the image below. Click the small arrow next to sorting option to toggle between ascending and descending sorting.


The list of responsibility displayed in the Responsibilities section represents the topics the contact is responsible for e.g. Reservation, General Questions, or Authorized Signatory. The selected responsibilities determine the initial selection of subscription in Subscriptions tab. **Please note that the function to send notifications as per the subscription is still under development. **
