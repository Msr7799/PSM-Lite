# YCS Companion App -- Manage your booking

Source: https://agodapropertypartnerhelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000345147-YCS-Companion-App-Manage-your-booking

Updated: 2021-09-26T07:24:25Z

In/Out function allows you to view the booking status for each day. You can see the number of guests expected to check in and out, as well as their booking details.

You can click on the date to see the number of arrivals and departures expected on that day of the week, or drill down to see the calendar to select the future date. The dates that have expected arrivals and departures are marked with violet and pink dots respectively. You can also click on each item to see the details of that booking.
