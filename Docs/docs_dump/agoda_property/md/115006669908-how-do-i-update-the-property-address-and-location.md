# How do I update the property address and location?

Source: https://agodapropertypartnerhelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/115006669908-How-do-I-update-the-property-address-and-location

Updated: 2021-07-15T02:27:34Z

**Overview**
**It's very important to provide correct address information of your property. This article will outline how to update address and location of your property.**
**Content **
This article includes
|


**How to change the property address in YCS?**

- Go to
** Settings** and click **Content**
- Select
** Basic Information**
-
**Change your street address** in English or your local language if available
- Click
**Save**


**How to change the property location in YCS?**

- Login to YCS
- Go to
**Settings** and click **Content**
- Select
**Map**
-
**Pin the location** on a map or provide latitude and longitude of your property
- Click
**Save**

**Frequently Asked Questions**

**I input latitude and longitude obtained online, why in your map it shows somewhere else?**

Different map may have different coordinates, we recommend you obtain them from Google Map, as we map service is based on Google Map.
