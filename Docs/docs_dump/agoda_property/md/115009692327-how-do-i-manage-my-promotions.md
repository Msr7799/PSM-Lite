# How do I manage my promotions?

Source: https://agodapropertypartnerhelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/115009692327-How-do-I-manage-my-promotions

Updated: 2021-09-29T08:43:20Z

##
|


You can go to YCS, in **Manage** Tab and click **Promotions**.



The basic requirements are **Sale dates**, **Stay dates** and a **discount**. There are more functions that can be applied. Find more about Features and Definitions here



At the right top corner of your active promotion card, you will see options to edit, duplicate or delete the promotion. You can also click right on the promotion card to edit.




The expired promotion will be located at **Expired / Missing promotions** section.** **Click to extend will reactivate the promotion. "Forever" sale date and stay date will be preselected.


**When will the promotion take effective?**

After created, your promotion will instantly display on the website and available to the customers based on the criteria you provided.

**How does the cancellation policy in promotion works?**

Promotion's cancellation policy overwrites all the other cancellation policy.

**Can I add restriction to promotions?**

Blackout days, cancellation policy and other restrictions can be added in **Add a restriction **section
