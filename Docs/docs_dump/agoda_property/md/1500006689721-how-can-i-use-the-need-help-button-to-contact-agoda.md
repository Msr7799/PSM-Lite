# How can I use the Need Help button to contact Agoda?

Source: https://agodapropertypartnerhelp.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500006689721-How-can-I-use-the-Need-Help-button-to-contact-Agoda

Updated: 2021-10-02T08:53:56Z

##
|

Submitting a request via Help widget in YCS is the easiest way to raise your questions or problems encountered. Our Agoda accommodation support is always ready to help!

1.Go to YCS

2. Find *Need Help* button located at the bottom right corner.

3. Click **New Support Request**


4.Fill in the form for us to verify your property as well as to know more about your concerns/issues encountered. Please fill this form right to get a quicker reply.
