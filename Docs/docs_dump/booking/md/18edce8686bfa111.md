# Uploading, deleting, reordering, and editing photos

You can add, update, and replace photos in your property’s photo gallery on the Extranet or Pulse app. Keep in mind that it takes time for photos to be updated correctly to your property page.

Uploading videos in your property’s photo gallery isn’t possible.

**In this article:**

**Adding new photos**

Follow these steps to add new photos to your gallery on the Extranet:

Follow these steps to add new 360° photos on the Extranet:

- Log in to the
**Extranet** - Click
**Property**, then**Photos** - Under the Main gallery section, you’ll find a new section,
**Introducing 360° Photos on your Property Page** - Click
**Add photos**

Continue with the following steps to add photos to a particular room or unit. Make sure your room or unit photo galleries only show facilities found in that room or unit type.

- Select the room or unit type
- Select
**Add photos**

Follow these steps to add new photos through the Pulse app:

- Log in to the Pulse app
- Tap
**More** - Tap
**Property details** - Select the property you want to add photos for – you can select more than one
- Tap
**Photos** - Select
**Photo Gallery**or**Room** - Tap
**Add photo**, then either**Take photo**to take one using your mobile device, or**Choose**to upload a photofrom your device’s gallery - Tap the check icon (
**✓**) to save your changes if you’re using Android, or tap**Save**if you’re using iOS

**Deleting photos**

Follow these steps to remove photos from your property page on the Extranet:

- Log in to the
**Extranet** - Click
**Property**, then**Photos** - Select the photo you’d like to remove – to remove multiple photos at once, select the photos and click
**Delete photos** - Click
**Options** - Click
**Delete**, then**OK**

Follow these steps to remove photos from your property on the Pulse app using an **iOS** device:

- Log in to the Pulse app
- Tap
**More** - Tap
**Property details**, then**Photos** - Select a room or unit
- Tap the photo you want to delete
- Tap the trash icon
- Tap
**Delete**

Follow these steps to remove photos from your property through Pulse on an **Android **device:

- Log in to Pulse
- Tap
**More** - Tap
**Property details**, then**Photos** - Tap the photo you want to delete
- Tap
**Delete photo** - Tap
**Delete**

**Changing the order of photos**

Follow these steps to change the photo order on your property page via the Extranet:

- Log in to the
**Extranet** - Click
**Property**, then**Photos** - Click and drag the photos to reorder them

Follow these steps to change the photo order in the Pulse app:

- Log in to the Pulse app
- Tap
**More** - Tap
**Property details** - Select the property you want to add photos for – you can select more than one
- Tap
**Photos** - Select
**Photo Gallery**or**Room** - Tap
**Rearrange photos**to drag and drop the photos into your preferred order - Tap the check icon (
**✓**) to save your changes if you’re using Android, or tap**Save**if you’re using iOS

Improve how your photos are displayed on your property page by using the **Smart ordering** feature. This feature uses machine learning to optimize the order of your photos in the best way possible for potential guests. Follow these steps to turn the feature on or off:

- Log in to the
**Extranet** - Click
**Property**, then**Photos** - Under
**Main gallery**, click the**Smart ordering**toggle button - Select
**Turn off**or**Keep on**

**Adding or changing the main photo**

Follow these steps to upload a new main photo via the Extranet:

- Log in to the
**Extranet** - Click
**Property**, then**Photos** - Click
**Add photos** - Select the photo, then click
**Options** - Click
**Set as preferred main photo**

If you already have multiple photos uploaded to your main gallery, follow these steps to set one as your main photo:

- Log in to the
**Extranet** - Click
**Property**, then**Photos** - Under
**Main gallery**, click and drag your chosen photo to the**Preferred main photo**spot

You can also add or change your main photo through the Pulse app – here’s how:

- Log in to the Pulse app
- Tap
**More** - Tap
**Property details** - Tap
**Property**– you can select more than one - Tap
**Photos**, then**Property gallery**– your current main photo will be shown first - Tap
**Add photo**to upload a new main photo from your device, or tap**Rearrange photos**to drag and drop an existing photo in your gallery to your main photo - Tap the check icon (
**✓**) to save your changes if you’re using Android, or tap**Save**if you’re using iOS

**Tagging photos**

Tags help you link your property to guest searches and can lead potential bookers to your property. Follow these steps to tag your photos:

- Log in to the
**Extranet** - Click
**Property**, then**Photos** - Select a photo, then click
**Options** - Click
**Edit or tag** - Select existing tags or create new tags
- Click
**Finish**

If you want to add a tag to your property that has a unit with a living room area, search for and add the tag "living room." Or, if your property has restaurant facilities, you can add the tag "restaurant" for the image of it.

**Rotating photos**

Follow these steps to rotate your photos:

- Log in to the
**Extranet** - Click
**Property**, then**Photos** - Select the photo you want to rotate, then click
**Edit** - Under the photo, choose to rotate your photo clockwise or counter-clockwise
- Click Save, then
**Finish**
