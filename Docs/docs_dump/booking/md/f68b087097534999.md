# Updating your property’s facilities


Follow these steps to update your property’s facilities on the Extranet:

- Log in to the
**Extranet**. - Under
**Property**, click**Facilities & services**. - Select the facilities and services you offer at your property.
- Click
**Save**.

For facilities that you’ve added to your listing, you can also include additional details and photos.

Here are some facilities you can add to your listing:

- Swimming pools, saunas, hot tubs, and jacuzzis
- Gyms and fitness centers
- Restaurants and bars
- Gardens and terraces
- Reception, shuttle, and front desk services
- Kids’ clubs and family entertainment
- Safety, security, cleanliness, and cleaning services
- Building information
- Business facilities
- Sustainability initiatives
- Car rental services

Note: If you want to provide specific details about your car rental services, contact the guests directly.


Follow these steps to update your property’s facilities using the Pulse app:

- Log in to Pulse on your mobile device.
- Tap
**More**. - If you manage multiple properties, select the property you want to update under
**Property details**. - Tap
**Facilities & services**. - Select the facilities and services you offer at your property.
- Give additional details if prompted.
- Tap
**Save**.
