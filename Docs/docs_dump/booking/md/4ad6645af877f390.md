# Payout statements for multi-property partners on Payments by Booking.com with a bank transfer payout: FAQs

-
A payout statement is a report of the sums you receive via bank transfer from us. It contains all the reservations and transactions that contribute to your payouts, as well as breakdowns of commission, taxes, and other items.

-
Payout statements are generated each time a bank transfer toward you is made. This is based on your payout timing.

-
Here’s how you can download your payout statements:

- Sign in to the group Extranet.
- Click the
**Finance**menu, then**Financial Report.** - Find the payout report you want to download.
- Click “select the data” in the drop-down to customize what information you want to be included in your report.
- Choose to download your report in either PDF, CSV, or XLS file format.

Add an email reminder for up to five other people in your organization to return to the Extranet to download the report.

-
Reservations that weren't paid through Payments by Booking.com. Reservations that were paid at the property or reservations collected directly by you won’t appear in this report.

-
A blank report means you didn’t receive a payout during the selected reporting period.
