# Editing bank and invoice details

- Sign in to the Extranet through your primary or admin account.
- Click the
**Finance**menu, then**Finance settings**. - Under the
**General finance**section, click**Invoice details**. - Click
**Edit details**to modify details including**Invoicing name**,**For the attention of**,**Address**,**Zip code**, and**City**. - Click
**Review changes**.

Note: Changes to your invoice details will be reflected in the next invoicing cycle.
